Policy Platform

Three Promises.
Zero Excuses.

01
Public Safety

Safer Streets

Los Santos is a city with a pulse — and that means everyone has a stake in how it runs. A Herbington administration won't silence the streets. Instead, we'll make sure every voice gets a seat at the table: PD, community, and yes, even those the system has written off. Reform starts with listening.

  • Create direct channels for both law enforcement and community voices to be heard at City Hall
  • Reform current charging practices — ensuring charges are fair, proportional, and consistently applied
  • Establish a civilian oversight board with real authority to review policing decisions
  • Partner with local organizations to address root causes — not just symptoms
02
Economy

Stronger Businesses

The economy of Los Santos runs on the hustle of everyday entrepreneurs. City Hall should be a partner to business owners, not an obstacle. Blaze will build an environment where local businesses can grow, hire, and thrive — starting with a full audit of every business currently operating in the city and a smarter tax structure that puts money back into the hands of those looking to build something.

  • Full audit of current businesses operating in Los Santos — transparency first
  • Restructure taxes to actively support new business startups and entrepreneurship
  • Cut red tape and streamline business licensing and permitting
  • Create a small business support fund for local entrepreneurs
  • Attract new investment while protecting existing local commerce
03
Reform

Big Changes to the System

The system as it stands works for the connected, the wealthy, and the powerful. Blaze is committed to breaking that cycle — bringing transparency, accountability, and fairness to every corner of city government.

  • Ethics reform for city council seats — accountability starts at the top
  • Open-door town halls in every district, every quarter
  • Whistleblower protections for city employees who speak up
